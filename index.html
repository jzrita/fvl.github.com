Yu Li

liyu[at]u.nus.edu


I got my PhD degree from School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS) in 2015, working with Prof. Michael S. Brown. My thesis has won the IDA medal (best thesis award). During 2014 to 2016, I had a great opportunity to work at Advanced Digital Sciences Center (ADSC), a research center founded by University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) and the Singapore Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR). My research interests are computer vision and computational photography.
